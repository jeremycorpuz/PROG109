About

Hi, I'm Jeremy, an aspiring graphic designer.

I was born in Vancouver, Canada, but grew up in Seattle for most of my life.

I decided to join this class because I was interested in the web development that goes hand in hand with UI/UX Design. As I'm steadily going into the graphic design field, basic knowledge of HTML & CSS would be a useful skill to have under my belt. I can better understand how aesthetic design is incorporated onto websites while catering to the accessibility and usabillity requirements that web developers take priority in. Accessibility & Usability was a significant concept to learn about, that although a website may be aesthetically appealing, the way users interact with the site is important to how they percieve and acquire information. I'm glad that I was able to learn the fundamentals and hope to continue developing these skills in order to create my own graphic design portfolio.

I'm passionate in anything that's creative whether it's painting, drawing, cinematography, etc. I also ocassionally pick up my guitar to pass time. If you're interested in looking at some work I've done, I've included recent pieces in my creative page. I am planning on pursing a bachelors in graphic design, aiming to attend RISD (Rhode Island School of Design) in Providence.
